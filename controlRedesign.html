Control Redesign
I was tasked with a redesign of the Lampat Pro Desk Lamp control. This control manipulated the color and intensity of the light, but failed to be intuitive to users - requiring minutes to figure out how to reach the desired lighting condition.

My goal was to provide an intuitive first-time user experience, putting the knowledge out into the world (instead of forcing users to remember arbitrary labels) and increasing the level of control users had over the lampâ€™s parameters.
