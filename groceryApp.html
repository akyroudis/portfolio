Grocery App
This app was born through the frustrations of grocery store users during Covid-times.

In order to cut down on grocery store outings, and minimize the amount of time spent in the store, we created an app to speed up the process of finding items and removed the need to wait in long checkout lines. Now users are able to navigate the grocery store with the most parsimonious path, scanning items as they go.
