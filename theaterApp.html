Theater App
Going to a theater to see a show is a fun experience that should not be hampered by menial stressors.

This app allows theater-goers to view a map of the theater, finding the quickest route to their seat. Additionally, the app allows users to check on the business of the concession stands and the bathrooms, so an educated decision can be made, and save precious time.
